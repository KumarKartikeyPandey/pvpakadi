Our Belief

P.V. Pakadi

Block Kapatanganj, District Kushinagar

Believes that true education must be life-sustaining and life empowering. We live our faith through a creative and competent actualization of a school culture that is broad in vision and embraces the multiplicity that characterizes our words. P.V. Pakadi is a wholesome, happy school with Indian values and an standard outlook.

From Principal's Desk

“Everyone has inside of him a piece of good news. The good news is that you don’t know how great you can be ! How much you can love ! What you can accomplish ! And what your potential is !”

-Anne Frank (aged 14 years) from ‘The Diary of a Young Girl’

Echoing the same sentiments, we at P.V. Pakadi, endeavour to raise happy, positive and balanced students and guide them to realize their highest potential.

We are committed to providing holistic education to the students under our care and in moulding them into learners for life. Our aim is to create curious, enquiring minds who eagerly explore ideas and concepts and can communicate them confidently and effectively. By providing them varied experiences and perspectives, we hope to develop our students into global citizens who appreciate differences and will nurture the planet and create a peaceful world. Our integration of values and morals into the everyday curriculum validates the emphasis we lay on creating principled citizens of the country. An amalgamation of the traditional with the modern, P.V. Pakadi stands as an institution of excellence where children learn and grow in the happy environment of the school everyday.

Our Facilities

Library

Discover, learn, and explore in our vibrant school library—your gateway to diverse books, digital resources, and collaborative study. Ignite curiosity today!

Mid-Day Meal

Nourishing minds, one meal at a time. Enjoy balanced and delicious midday meals, fostering health and energy for successful learning.

Indoor/Outdoor Activities

Experience the best of both worlds with our dynamic indoor-outdoor activities. Foster skills, teamwork, and well-rounded growth in engaging environments.

Learning Corner

Explore our dedicated learning corner—where knowledge comes to life. Engage with resources, collaborate, and expand horizons in a nurturing space.

Cost-Free Books & Uniform

Empowering every student, we provide cost-free books and uniforms, ensuring equal access to quality education and fostering inclusivity.

Health & Hygiene

Prioritizing well-being, our school upholds health and hygiene standards. A clean and safe environment nurtures optimal learning and growth.
